What is Mother's Day?

Mother's day is a holiday where you celebrate how wonderful your mom is. You give her a gift to show her just how much you appreciate everything she does for you. So go find your mother on this special day and tell her how much you love her.

Gifts you could give your mom

  1. Make her a card
  2. Write her a poem
  3. Ger her some flowers
