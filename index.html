Happy Mothers Day!

What is mothers day?

Mothers day is a day to celebrate your mom and everything she has done for you. It is a day to tell you mom just how much you appreciate and love her. Go find your mom on this very special day and give her a big hug to show how thankful you are for her.

Things to get your mom for mothers day

  1. Flowers
  2. A handmade card
  3. Chocolate
